# 🎥 ONBOARDING VIDEO SCRIPT — AusAI Tech

**Purpose:** 3-minute Loom welcome video you send to every new client after they sign.  
**When:** Within 24 hours of contract signing.  
**Tone:** Warm, professional, excited to work together.

> **Why video?** A 3-min face-to-camera video reduces "first week anxiety" by 80%. Clients see you as a person, not a faceless freelancer.

---

## THE 3-MINUTE SCRIPT

### 0:00–0:30 — THE WARM WELCOME

```
[ON CAMERA — smile, good lighting, quiet background]

"Hey {Client Name} — congrats on kicking off your automation project! 
I'm {Your Name}, and I just wanted to send a quick video to say thanks 
for trusting me with this, and to walk through what happens next."
```

**Why:** Personal, grateful, sets the "what happens next" hook.

---

### 0:30–1:00 — QUICK RECAP

```
[ON CAMERA]

"Just to make sure we're on the same page:
We're building {1-sentence project recap — what + outcome}.

The timeline is {X} business days, and when we're done, 
you'll save about {Y} hours per week on {specific task}.

Sound right?"
```

**Why:** Confirms scope. Makes them nod. Prevents "I thought you were building X."

---

### 1:00–1:45 — WHAT HAPPENS NEXT (The Roadmap)

```
[SCREEN SHARE — show PROJECT_MANAGEMENT_SOP.md phase tracker or a simple timeline]

"Here's the plan:
Week 1: I'll map your current workflow and set up the tools.
Week 2: Build + test the first version. You'll get a Loom walkthrough.
Week 3: We'll review together, make any tweaks, and go live.
Week 4: I'll hand over documentation + a 5-min walkthrough video.

I'll send you a Loom update every {Tuesday/Thursday} so you always 
know where things are.

No need to micromanage — I've got this. But if you ever have 
questions, just reply to any Loom or Slack me. I respond same-day."
```

**Why:** Removes uncertainty ("what's happening week 2?"). Sets communication expectations.

---

### 1:45–2:30 — "WHAT I NEED FROM YOU"

```
[ON CAMERA]

"Two quick things I need from you this week:

1. {Access Item — Google account, API key, login to X}
   I've sent a secure link to collect these. Takes 2 minutes.

2. {Decision — e.g., "Which Slack channel should alerts go to?"}
   Just reply to this video with your answer.

That's it. Everything else is on me."
```

**Why:** Minimal asks. Clear. Actionable. They reply with the answer = engagement hook.

---

### 2:30–3:00 — THE CLOSE

```
[ON CAMERA — genuine smile]

"I'm genuinely excited about this project. 
{Why you're excited — specific to them or their industry}.

If you need anything at all, I'm a Slack message away.

Talk soon, {Client Name}!"

[WAVE or thumbs up]
```

**Why:** Ends on warmth. Reinforces availability.

---

## CUSTOMISATION PER CLIENT TYPE

| Client Type | Customise This Part |
|---|---|
| **First-time automation buyer** | Spend extra time on roadmap (they're nervous). Add: "You might feel like nothing's happening in Week 1 — that's normal. I'm mapping and planning." |
| **Technical founder** | Speed up the roadmap. Add: "You'll probably want to see the architecture doc — I'll share it by Day 3." |
| **Busy CEO** | Shorten to 2 min. Cut the recap. Lead with: "Two things I need from you, then I disappear and deliver." |
| **Referral** | Add: "And thanks to {Referrer Name} for connecting us — they're one of my favourite clients." |

---

## RECORDING CHECKLIST

- [ ] **Lighting:** Face well-lit (window light or ring light). No backlight.
- [ ] **Audio:** Clear. No echo, no fan noise, no keyboard clicking.
- [ ] **Background:** Clean, professional. Bookshelf, plain wall, or blurred.
- [ ] **Camera:** Eye level. Look at the lens, not the screen.
- [ ] **Smile:** Genuine. You're talking to a person who just paid you.
- [ ] **Energy:** Slightly more energetic than normal conversation (video flattens energy).
- [ ] **Length:** Under 3:30. Re-record if over 4 min.
- [ ] **Test watch:** Watch it back at 1.5× speed (that's how busy clients watch).
