# Local SEO Guide — AusAI Tech (Australian Focus)

> **Purpose:** Australian-specific local SEO to attract "AI automation consultant [city]" searches. Passive inbound lead generation that compounds over time.
> **When to use:** Set up once, maintain monthly. Results start showing in 2-6 months.
> **Prerequisite:** Website deployed (GitHub Pages or custom domain), Google account.

---

## The Local SEO Philosophy

You don't need to rank for "AI automation" globally. You need to rank for "AI automation consultant Melbourne" (or your city). Local intent = high purchase intent. Someone searching "AI consultant near me" is ready to buy.

**Goal:** When a Brisbane business owner Googles "automation consultant," you're in the top 3 results (Google Business Profile + organic).

---

## Phase 1: Google Business Profile (30 min, Once)

This is your single highest-ROI SEO action.

### Setup

1. Go to `business.google.com` → Create profile
2. Business name: **AusAI Tech — AI Automation & Security Consulting**
3. Category: **"Automation Consultant"** (primary) + **"Business Management Consultant"** (secondary)
4. Address: Use your home address or a virtual office. Choose "Service area business" if you don't have a physical office
5. Service area: Your city + 50km radius (e.g., "Melbourne" and surrounding suburbs)
6. Phone: Your business number
7. Website: Your deployed GitHub Pages URL or custom domain
8. Hours: Mon-Fri 9:00-17:00

### Optimisation

| Element | What to Do |
|---|---|
| **Description** | 750 characters. Include: "AI automation consultant [city]. We build custom automations for small businesses — customer support, data entry, reporting. A$497 AI Audit available. Based in [suburb]." |
| **Services** | List: AI Audit (A$497), AI Build (A$1,497), AI Partner Retainer (A$2,497/mo), Custom Automation, Security Audit |
| **Photos** | Add 5+ photos: logo, portfolio screenshots, before/after demo screenshots, a professional headshot |
| **Posts (weekly)** | Use the template below. Google rewards active profiles. |
| **Q&A** | Pre-seed 3 questions: "How much does an AI audit cost?", "What tools do you use?", "Do you work with [industry] businesses?" Answer them yourself. |
| **Reviews** | Aim for 5+ reviews in the first 3 months. Ask every happy client: "If you've got 2 minutes, a Google review helps enormously." |

### Weekly Google Business Profile Post Template

```
TITLE: {Question or benefit — e.g., "Is your team spending 10+ hours/week on data entry?"}

BODY: {3-4 sentences — tip, insight, or client result. End with soft CTA.}

EXAMPLE: "Most small businesses we audit are losing 10-20 hours/week on manual tasks that AI can handle. Last month we built an automation for a Melbourne e-commerce business that cut their reporting time from 15 hours to 15 minutes. Wondering if automation makes sense for you? Book a free 30-min call."

BUTTON: "Book Free Audit" → Link to Calendly
```

## Phase 2: On-Page SEO (Website) (2 Hours, Once)

### Homepage Optimisation

- **Title tag:** "AI Automation Consultant {City} | AusAI Tech — A$497 AI Audits"
- **Meta description:** "Melbourne AI automation consultant. We build custom automations for small businesses — n8n, Zapier, AI chatbots. Free 30-min audit available. 30-day warranty on all builds."
- **H1:** "AI Automation Consultant {City} — Stop Losing 10+ Hours/Week to Manual Work"
- **H2s:** "What We Automate" / "How It Works" / "Pricing" / "Results"
- **Body:** Include "city" 3-5× naturally in copy. Mention suburbs: "serving Melbourne, Richmond, South Yarra, and surrounding areas."

### Location Pages (If Serving Multiple Cities)

Create one page per city:
- `{domain}/ai-automation-consultant-melbourne/`
- `{domain}/ai-automation-consultant-sydney/`
- `{domain}/ai-automation-consultant-brisbane/`

Each page: unique content (don't copy-paste). Local references (mention local landmarks, suburbs, business culture).

### Technical SEO

- [ ] HTTPS enabled (GitHub Pages does this automatically)
- [ ] Mobile-friendly (test with Google's Mobile-Friendly Test)
- [ ] Page speed < 3 seconds (GitHub Pages is fast; keep images under 200KB)
- [ ] Sitemap.xml submitted to Google Search Console
- [ ] robots.txt allows indexing
- [ ] Schema markup: "LocalBusiness" schema with your city, phone, services
