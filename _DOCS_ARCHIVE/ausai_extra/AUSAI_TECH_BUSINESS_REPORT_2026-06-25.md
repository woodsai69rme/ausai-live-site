## 1. Executive Summary

AusAI Tech is an Australian AI agency offering workflow automation, AI media production, local AI deployment, security auditing, and consulting services. The business leverages a fully operational technical stack (ComfyUI, n8n, Ollama, OpenRouter, Supabase, FastAPI) already deployed on-premises with zero cloud dependency.

**Current stage:** Pre-revenue, bootstrapped, zero-cost infrastructure.  
**Target market:** Australian SMBs ($50K–$5M revenue) adopting AI for the first time.  
**Business model:** Project-based + retainer, transitioning to SaaS in Month 6+.
