## Step 1: Create Account (10 min)

1. Go to https://fiverr.com
2. Click "Join" → Use email or Google
3. Click "Become a Seller" in top-right menu
4. Fill in profile:
   - **Full Name**: Your real name
   - **Profile Photo**: Professional headshot
   - **Description**: "AI Automation Specialist based in Australia. I build custom ComfyUI workflows, generate AI music videos, and deploy local AI systems. 50+ workflows built on RTX 4060 hardware."
   - **Languages**: English (Native/Bilingual)
   - **Skills**: ComfyUI, AI Video Generation, Stable Diffusion, n8n, Python, Ollama, Workflow Automation
5. Click "Continue"

## Portfolio Images — How to Get Them

You need 3 images per gig (6 total, can reuse some):

### For Gig 1 (Music Video):
1. **Dark/moody AI video frame** — cinematic, neon-lit
2. **Colorful/abstract AI frame** — bright, artistic
3. **Character-focused AI frame** — consistent character

### For Gig 2 (ComfyUI Workflow):
1. **Clean workflow screenshot** — ComfyUI canvas with organized nodes
2. **Output image** — the result the workflow produces
3. **Before/after comparison** — input vs. output side-by-side

### How to create them:

**Option A: Use your own ComfyUI (once models download)**
1. Open `http://127.0.0.1:8188`
2. Load Juggernaut-XL checkpoint
3. Generate images with cinematic prompts
4. Screenshot the workflow + save outputs

**Option B: Quick placeholder images**
1. Go to https://lexica.art or https://civitai.com
2. Search "cinematic AI art" or "cyberpunk"
3. Download 3-6 high-quality AI images
4. Use these as temporary portfolio until you generate your own
5. **Note**: Be transparent if asked — these are examples of the style you can produce

**Option C: Generate via free online tools**
1. Go to https://huggingface.co/spaces/stabilityai/stable-diffusion
2. Generate free images with prompts like:
   - "cinematic AI technology office, modern minimalist, warm lighting, 4k, photorealistic"
   - "abstract neon music visualization, purple and blue, digital art"
   - "futuristic cyberpunk character, detailed face, consistent style"

## Profile Optimization Tips

- **Response Time**: Set to "Within 1 hour" (Fiverr boosts fast responders)
- **Availability**: Turn on "Available Now" during AEST business hours
- **First Orders**: Consider pricing lower (A$50-100) for first 3 orders to get reviews
- **Briefs**: Check daily — Fiverr matches you with buyers automatically based on your gigs (free leads)
- **Keywords**: Ensure "AI", "ComfyUI", "Stable Diffusion", "Workflow" appear in gig titles

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| No orders after 2 weeks | Lower prices 20%, update gig images, check buyer requests |
| Low impressions | Update tags, rewrite first 2 lines of description with keywords |
| Bad review | Offer free revision, resolve within 24h |
| Payment issues | Check PayPal is verified, withdrawal minimum is $20 |
