# LinkedIn DM Templates — first 5 sends today

> Five templates aligned to the top-5 revenue paths from
> `PRODUCTIVITY_AND_REVENUE_OPTIONS.md`. Each is <300 chars, no pitches,
> sends in ≤90 seconds from your side.

## ⚠️  FILL-IN CHECKLIST BEFORE SENDING (do not skip)

Every `{placeholder}` MUST be replaced before you paste into LinkedIn.
A sent message literally containing `{company}` is worse than no message.

| Placeholder              | Replace with                                      |
|--------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| `{first_name}`           | Their LinkedIn first name                         |
| `{company}`              | Their company (e.g. "BindiMaps")                  |
| `{your_name}`            | Your real name (no aliases)                       |
| `{track}` / `{n}`        | Literal song / number, no placeholder             |
| `{flow}`                 | One specific automation you noticed they run     |
| `{practice}`             | Their practice type ("dental clinic", "law firm") |
| `{link}` / `{calendly-link}` | A real URL you control                       |

**Quick Find/Replace recipe (Ctrl+H in Notepad++):**
1. Open this file
2. Ctrl+H
3. Search: `{first_name}` → Replace: (paste their name)
4. Repeat for `{company}`, `{your_name}`
5. Copy the now-clean block; paste into LinkedIn

## How to send

1. Open LinkedIn → search the **company name** (see targeting below) → find
   the founder / marketing lead / head of product → click Connect → in the
   note paste the matching template below.
2. After they accept, send a 2-line follow-up offering the free sample.
3. Use `tools/07_archive_overlapping_orchestrators.bat /UNDO` if you need
   to dive back into your empire instead.

## Why this works

- Templates are **<300 chars** — fits LinkedIn's connect-note limit (300).
- They **offer a free asset** before the price — reciprocity.
- They **name a concrete outcome** ("A$200 / 48hr / 2 revisions").
- They **don't ask for the meeting** in the first note — only the asset.
- They **target via company-name first** so you're known before you write.

If 25 sends → 5 conversations → 1 gig closed:
**expected first-month revenue = A$200–10,000** (varies wildly by Path).
