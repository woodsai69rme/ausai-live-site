1. Scope of Ongoing Services

This agreement covers maintenance, monitoring, and light enhancement of the systems delivered under the original Statement of Work. The following services are included in the monthly retainer:

1.1 Included (covered by monthly fee)

  • System monitoring: Weekly health checks of deployed workflows, APIs, and automations
  • Bug fixes: Resolution of defects in delivered code at no additional charge
  • Minor tweaks: Up to 2 hours/month of small adjustments (prompt tuning, filter changes, routing rules)
  • Security patches: Critical dependency updates and security advisory monitoring
  • Monthly status report: One-page PDF summarising uptime, changes made, and recommendations
  • Response time SLA: Acknowledgement within 24 hours on business days; resolution target 3 business days for P2 issues, 1 business day for P1 (system down)

1.2 Not Included (billed separately)

  • New feature development or integrations not in the original scope
  • Major model migrations (e.g., switching from GPT-4 to Claude 3.5)
  • Data migration or re-training on new datasets
  • Work exceeding 2 hours/month of minor tweaks (billed at agreed hourly rate)
  • Third-party API costs (OpenRouter, Supabase, etc.) — these are passed through at cost + 10% admin fee
