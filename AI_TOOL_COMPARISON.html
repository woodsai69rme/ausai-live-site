Pick Zapier if...

You need one or two simple automations. "When I get a new Gmail, send me a Slack message." You don't want to think about hosting or code. You're okay with paying as you scale.

Best for: Beginners & Simple Tasks

Pick Make if...

You need multi-step workflows with data transformation. You want more power than Zapier but don't want to self-host. You need the larger integration library (1,500+ apps).

Best for: Power Users & Data Flows

Pick n8n if...

You want maximum power at minimum cost. You're comfortable with a steeper learning curve. You need AI/LLM integration, error handling, and self-hosting for data control. This is what we recommend for most businesses.

Best for: Most Businesses ★ Our Recommendation

Pick a Custom Build if...

You have HIPAA/compliance requirements. You need real-time sub-second processing. You're handling 1M+ operations/month. Your workflow is too complex for visual editors. This is more expensive but infinitely flexible.

Best for: Enterprise & Compliance
