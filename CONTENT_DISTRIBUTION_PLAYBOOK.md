# 📡 CONTENT DISTRIBUTION PLAYBOOK — AusAI Tech

**Purpose:** Get your expertise in front of other people's audiences — guest posts AND podcast appearances.  
**Goal:** 1 guest post/month + 1 podcast appearance/quarter.  
**Why both:** Guest posts build SEO backlinks. Podcasts build personal authority. They feed each other.

## 1. GUEST POSTS

### Pitch Email A — "I Wrote Something Your Readers Need"

```
Subject: Guest post idea: {Topic} — {specific_angle}

Hi {editor_name},

I'm an AI automation consultant who helps businesses save 15+ hrs/week 
through automation. I've been reading {blog_name} for {time} — 
your article on {relevant_article} was spot-on.

I'd love to contribute a guest post: "{proposed_title}"

It would cover:
→ {Point 1}
→ {Point 2}
→ {Point 3}

My recent writing: {link_to_linkedin_article}

Let me know if this fits your calendar.

{Your Name}
```

### Pitch Email B — "Trend Reaction"

```
Subject: {Trend} is changing automation — want a take on it?

Hi {editor_name},

{1-sentence trend observation}. I've been implementing this for clients 
and have a contrarian take your readers might find interesting.

Guest post pitch: "{title}"

Happy to send a draft for your review. No hard feelings if it's not a fit.

{Your Name}
```

### Article Template

```
Title: How to {Automate X} in {Time} — {Result hook}

HOOK (2-3 sentences):
"Every week, business owners lose {N} hours to {manual_task}. 
Here's how to reclaim that time with a {time_to_setup} automation."

THE PROBLEM (1 paragraph):
Why manual {task} doesn't scale.

THE SOLUTION (3-5 steps):
Step 1: {Tool + trigger}
Step 2: {Action}
Step 3: {Result}
[Screenshots]

THE RESULTS:
Specific numbers. "A {industry} client saved {X} hrs/week."

CTA:
"Try this with the free tier of {tool}. Takes {time} to set up."
```

## 2. PODCAST PITCHES

### Pitch Email A — "Specific Value"

```
Subject: Podcast idea: How a {industry} business saved 22 hrs/week with AI

Hi {host_name},

I'm an AI automation consultant who helps AU businesses eliminate 
repetitive work. Your episode with {previous_guest} was great — 
especially the part about {specific_moment}.

I'd love to share a concrete story your listeners can use:

"How a {industry} client automated {process} and saved 22 hrs/week 
— the exact Zapier/n8n workflow, step by step."

Happy to send talking points or jump on a quick call.

{Your Name}
{Your LinkedIn}
```

### Pitch Email B — "Trend"

```
Subject: 3 AI automation trends AU businesses are missing in 2026

Hi {host_name},

Most Australian small businesses are still doing {manual_task} by hand. 
The ones who automate are pulling ahead — fast.

I'd love to share 3 trends + specific tools your listeners can 
implement this week, based on my work with {N} AU businesses.

Topics we could cover:
→ {Topic 1}
→ {Topic 2}
→ {Topic 3}

No sales pitch — just actionable insights.

{Your Name}
```
