# 🎁 CLIENT GIFT GUIDE — AusAI Tech

**Purpose:** When, what, and how to gift clients — building relationships within ATO-compliant boundaries.  
**Budget:** A$30–A$100 per gift.  
**Frequency:** 2-4 gifts/year per active client.

> **Why gifts work:** A thoughtful A$40 gift generates more goodwill than a A$200 discount. Gifts signal "I value this relationship beyond the transaction."

## 1. GIFT OCCASIONS

| Occasion | Timing | Appropriate Gift Tier |
|---|---|---|
| **Project completion** | Within 1 week of handoff | Small (A$20–A$50) |
| **Client's business milestone** (funding, anniversary, award) | Within 3 days of announcement | Small–Medium (A$30–A$70) |
| **End of year / holiday** | First 2 weeks of December | Medium (A$50–A$100) |
| **Referral thank-you** | Within 1 week of referred client signing | Medium (A$50–A$100) |
| **Client's birthday** (only if you know it) | Day of or day before | Small (A$20–A$40) |
| **Apology / service recovery** | Within 24 hrs of issue | Small–Medium (A$30–A$70) |

### Tier Guidelines

- **Green clients** (21-25 on `CLIENT_HEALTH_SCORECARD.md`): Gift at all appropriate occasions
- **Yellow clients** (16-20): Gift at project completion + end of year
- **Orange/Red clients** (≤15): No gifts — fix the relationship first

## 3. ATO TAX RULES (AUSTRALIA)

### When Gifts Are Tax-Deductible

Gifts to clients are deductible if they're:
- **Related to earning assessable income** (maintaining business relationships)
- **Not entertainment** (see below)
- **Reasonable in value** (ATO doesn't specify a hard limit, but <A$300 is generally safe)

### Entertainment vs. Non-Entertainment

| Gift Type | Classification | Deductible? |
|---|---|---|
| Hamper, wine, chocolates | Non-entertainment | ✅ Yes |
| Book, stationery, plant | Non-entertainment | ✅ Yes |
| Movie/sports tickets | Entertainment | ❌ Not deductible |
| Restaurant meal with client | Entertainment | ❌ Not deductible |
| Holiday accommodation | Entertainment | ❌ Not deductible |

> **Rule of thumb:** If you consume it together OR it's a leisure activity → entertainment (not deductible). If it's a physical item they take home → non-entertainment (deductible).

### FBT (Fringe Benefits Tax)

- **Gifts to clients:** No FBT (FBT only applies to employees)
- **Gifts to contractors/subcontractors:** May attract FBT if >A$300. Keep under A$300.
- **Record-keeping:** Keep receipts. Note the client name and business purpose on each.

### GST

- If the gift includes GST (most do) and you're GST-registered, you can claim the GST credit.
- If you're not GST-registered, the full amount is deductible as a business expense.

## 4. WHAT NOT TO GIFT

| Don't Gift... | Why |
|---|---|
| **Cash** | Feels like a bribe. ATO scrutiny. |
| **Anything alcohol-related to non-drinkers** | Know your client. Ask or research first. |
| **Overly personal items** (clothing, perfume) | Creepy. Too intimate for business relationships. |
| **Your company SWAG** (stress balls, cheap pens) | Not a gift. That's marketing. Gift them something for THEM. |
| **Gifts during active negotiations** | Can be perceived as bribery. Wait until after contract signed. |
| **Anything over A$300 without checking** | May trigger FBT or client's own gift policy concerns. |

## 5. GIFTING WORKFLOW

### The 3-Step Gift Process

```
1. Choose → 2. Personalise → 3. Deliver
```

**Step 1: Choose** using the occasion + budget guide above. When in doubt, ask: "If someone gave ME this, would I feel valued or advertised-to?"

**Step 2: Personalise** — the note matters more than the gift.
```
Handwritten note template:

{Client Name},

Working with {Company} on {specific project or quality} 
has been one of the highlights of my year.

{1 specific thing they did that you appreciate}
{e.g., "Your clarity on what you needed made building the automation a joy."}

Thank you for trusting me with your business.

Here's to more automation wins in 2026.

{Your Name}
```

**Step 3: Deliver**
- **Australia Post** for physical items
- **Hand-deliver** if you're meeting them anyway (lunch, office visit)
- **Digital gift cards:** Email with personal note (not auto-generated template)
- **Never send without a note.** A gift without a note feels transactional.
